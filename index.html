Why do nutritional monitoring?

There is no perfect diet, but it is essential to find the most suitable for each person at each particular moment of his life. My service is aimed at people who do not feel satisfied with their body and who want to find a balance through a path of personalized slimming and constant motivation.

Learn More

Nutritional Consultation - First visit

The first visit lasts about an hour, in which personal anamnesis, evaluation of eating habits, weight, height, body mass index, body composition by bioimpedance, structuring a personalized food plan.

Check-Up Appointment

Control visits are planned according to the objectives and needs of the person. During these tests, the measurements of the weight and the circumferences will be repeated and the bioimpedance analysis will be carried out again, in order to verify the progress together.

Body Composition

Body composition assessment includes circumference measurement and bioelectric impedance analysis (BIA), a fast and non-invasive method that allows the evaluation of body water, lean mass and fat mass.

About me

I graduated in 2007, and I have worked more than 15 years, accompaning particular patients, but also have worked in company, orienting employees and in the hospitalar environment.

The Process

I offer personal and online 1-1 consultations, starting with 1 hour sessions and continue with 30-minute sessions once the concepts are understood. The number of follow-ups required will depend on your goals, current knowledge and how fast you apply the recommendations.

Mission & Values

It has always been my passion to help people develop habits that benefit them. We take a motivational and non-judgmental approach to help you change your habits and find a strategy that works for you.

Achieving Go

I am passionate and constantly looking for a healthy lifestyle and I strongly believe that health can be structured first of all at the table. This is why my goal is to guide you with patience and experience in choosing the right food for you.
